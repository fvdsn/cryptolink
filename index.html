What's CryptoLink ?

CryptoLink allows you to encrypt and share messages, documents, and pictures. You just have to type your message, pick a password, and cryptolink will give you a link containing your now protected data.

You can then send this link by email, Facebook, Twitter, etc. Your recipients then click the link and provide the password. For those without the password reading your message is however impossible. And by impossible I mean even for the most ressourceful organisations.

Or you could save the link in your favorites — so you can easily access it later — and protect its content from phone or laptop thiefs. Or you could print the generated QR-Code, and store valuable information in your wallet or paper documents.

And the best part is that Cryptolink doesn't host or know anything about your messages. This means I can let you do whatever you want with CryptoLink. And you can rest assured that as long as you have the link, you have your data.

How secure is CryptoLink?

The data is encrypted with AES, one of the most secure encryption algorithm available. If you follow the password checker's recommendations, your data will be protected for the next century.

Sadly, CryptoLink does not yet support HTTPS. Without it, an attacker could provide you a fake version of cryptolink when you visit this website, and have access to all your data.

Supporting HTTPS is very simple; it only requires me to spend money. That I don't have. Thus if you find CryptoLink interesting or useful, please make a donation, any amount will help.
