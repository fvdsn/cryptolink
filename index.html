What is CryptoLink ?

CryptoLink is a open source web application that allows you to easily encrypt and share text messages, websites and pictures with a high level of security and privacy.

More specifically, CryptoLink encrypts the data into a link (or URL). All you have to do to decrypt your data is to follow the link and provide your password. There is nothing to install, and it works on almost every device.

Cryptolink doesn't know anything about your message. It doesn't host it, it doesn't know you encrypted it and neither does it know when or if you decrypt it. This means we can let you do whatever you want with CryptoLink and you can rest assured that as long as you have the link, you have your data.

How secure are cryptolinks?

First, keep in mind that CryptoLink is beta software with all the usual warnings it implies.

Cryptolinks are encrypted with AES, one of the most secure encryption algorithm available. But good encryption does not protect against easily guessable passwords; with the help of a supercomputer, a hacker can guess millions of passwords a second. It is therefore important for you to choose a good password. For this CryptoLink incorporates a very powerful password strength checker that gives solid advice. that you should follow.

How much data can I put in a cryptolink?

Most browsers allows you to put several megabytes of data inside a link, and that is enough for a lot of applications. The current bottleneck is the speed of the compression algorithm, where there is much room for improvement.

There is unfortunately much less room inside a QR-Code.
